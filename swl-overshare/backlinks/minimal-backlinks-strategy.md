# Minimal Targeted Backlinks Strategy

## Philosophy

Snow White Laundry will pursue backlinks with the same intention and restraint that defines the restaurant itself. Quality over quantity. Relevance over reach. Authentic connections over manufactured links.

## Target Categories

### 1. Local Newfoundland Directories
- **St. John's Tourism Directory**
- **Newfoundland and Labrador Restaurant Association**
- **Local Business Directories**
- **Chamber of Commerce Listings**

**Approach:** Simple, factual listings. No promotional language.

### 2. Culinary and Hospitality Publications
- **Canadian Restaurant News** (editorial pitch)
- **Eater Canada** (opening announcement)
- **The Globe and Mail Food Section** (philosophy piece)
- **Local food blogs** (ingredient-focused stories)

**Approach:** Pitch stories about philosophy, not promotion. Focus on intention, emotion, craft.

### 3. Professional Networks
- **LinkedIn Articles** (thought leadership)
- **Chef Forums** (craft discussions)
- **Hospitality Groups** (philosophy sharing)

**Approach:** Contribute value, not self-promotion. Share insights, not announcements.

### 4. Community Platforms
- **Reddit r/newfoundland** (authentic engagement)
- **Local Facebook Groups** (community participation)
- **Nextdoor** (neighborhood connection)

**Approach:** Participate genuinely. Answer questions. Share knowledge. Link only when relevant.

## Ethical Guidelines

1. **No Paid Links:** Never purchase backlinks
2. **No Link Farms:** Avoid directories that exist only for SEO
3. **No Manipulation:** Don't create fake accounts or personas
4. **No Spam:** Don't post links in irrelevant contexts
5. **Value First:** Always contribute value before linking

## Local Focus

Prioritize local backlinks over national or international:

- **St. John's Business Directories**
- **Newfoundland Tourism Sites**
- **Local News Outlets**
- **Community Organizations**
- **Regional Food Blogs**

## Success Criteria

A successful backlink strategy results in:

1. **Quality over Quantity:** 20-30 high-quality backlinks preferred over 100+ low-quality
2. **Relevance:** Links from contextually relevant sources
3. **Organic Growth:** Natural link acquisition over time
4. **Local Authority:** Strong local backlink profile
5. **Industry Recognition:** Links from respected culinary/hospitality sources

## Notes

- **Patience Required:** Backlinks take time to accumulate
- **Quality Matters:** One high-quality link beats ten low-quality
- **Authenticity Essential:** Forced links damage reputation
- **Local First:** Local backlinks often more valuable than distant ones
- **Value First:** Always contribute before asking

This strategy ensures that Snow White Laundry builds a backlink profile that reflects the restaurant's values: intentional, authentic, and focused on quality over quantity.
