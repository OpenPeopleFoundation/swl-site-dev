# Pre-Service Preparation at Snow White Laundry

## Summary
Pre-service preparation at Snow White Laundry is treated as the foundation of smooth service execution. The restaurant invests time and attention in thorough preparation, recognizing that what happens before service determines what is possible during service. Preparation is not rushed or improvised; it is systematic, documented, and refined.

Preparation covers multiple areas: ingredient prep, station setup, equipment checks, service materials, staff briefing. Each area is addressed with intention and attention to detail. Nothing is left to chance. The goal is to enter service with everything in place, allowing staff to focus on execution rather than improvisation.

The preparation process is documented and taught consistently. New staff learn not just what to prepare, but why it matters and how to do it efficiently. Preparation becomes a ritual that sets the tone for service, creating calm and confidence.

## Core Principles
- Thorough preparation enables smooth service execution.
- Preparation is systematic, not improvised.
- Every element of service requires preparation.
- Preparation is documented and taught consistently.
- Time invested in preparation pays dividends during service.

## Signals & Behaviours
- Prep lists are followed systematically.
- Stations are set up completely before service.
- Equipment is checked and ready.
- Staff briefings cover service details and expectations.
- Service begins with everything in place.
