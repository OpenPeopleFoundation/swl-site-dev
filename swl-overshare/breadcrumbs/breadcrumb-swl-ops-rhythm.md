# Operational Rhythm at Snow White Laundry

## Summary
Operational rhythm at Snow White Laundry is the pattern of how days, services, and seasons flow. The restaurant treats rhythm as a form of infrastructure: a stable pattern that gives staff and guests a sense of predictability.

Pre‑service, service, and post‑service rituals are designed as repeatable loops that make learning and improvement easier over time.

## Core Principles
- There should be a visible difference between preparation, execution, and recovery modes.
- Meetings and debriefs are short, focused, and regular.
- Rhythm is adjusted thoughtfully when data or staff feedback justifies it.

## Signals & Behaviours
- Service start times, briefings, and cut‑offs follow a consistent pattern.
- New staff can learn the weekly rhythm quickly.
- Changes to rhythm are documented and reviewed.
